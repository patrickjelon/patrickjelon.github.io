About the Author

Plethora Annings and Jason Head, two first world citizens striving to achieve fulfillment and productivity, while intelligently trying to build their lives free from toxic financial strain and stress.
They soon realized the goal of building a healthy life free from financial duress is full to the brim with impossibilities some seen most unforeseen. This reality led the authors to start writing about these difficulties to assist others in identifying similar conditions in order to help the average person solidify indemnifiers. Until one identifies the challenges, one is incapable of accepting the reality of these challenges thus prohibiting the ability to create solutions. Skummy Life, written by the authors in order to assist the average person, by helping that person realize their instincts have always been accurate. The authors are two average people, living in the same world you live in, going through the same struggles. Two people who decided to do something about the fixed game we are all living, and the fixed rules we are all playing.
Skummy Life, the truth behind your failure.
