About us

Say It With Fruit provides nutritious fruit platters that are almost too beautiful to eat. Customers can be confident that our company will go out of its way to get the best fresh fruit for their dessert. In order to personalize their event we never makes two exactly the same platters. Each one of them is customized with customer's individuality in mind. It makes our customers feel special and important.

Serving edible art to her customers makes her job enjoyable and satisfying.
She loves to create something personal for each customer TO MAKE EVERYONE FEEL SPECIAL.

History of Fruit Carving
Vegetable and fruit carving originates in Thailand in the 14th century. It is taught from the age of 11 in primary schools through secondary school in Thailand. In the mid 20th Century, the art of vegetable and fruit carving began to grow outside Thailand. Fruit and Vegetable carving is generally used as a garnish. It can also be used for flower arranging.
A good example of popularity of fruit and vegetable carvings are resorts in tourist destinations and cruises. Many of them use carving to garnish food and decorate the buffets.
