Our Services

Our pitch-polymer emulsion will create a stable and waterproof barrier that is non-leaching, binding road and surface effectively. If you need to stabilize founations or roads and road shoulders, you may require a service like this.

Soil stabilization may be an important element of your project, as without it other elements may not be able to proceed. Stablizing the soil around foundations and on roads are also necessary before paving over it or continuing construction.
