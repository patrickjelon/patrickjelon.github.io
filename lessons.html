What are our fruit carving lessons?

At our carving lessons we teach the technique of professional fruit and vegetable carving. You will gain skills to create a masterpiece using ordinary fruit an vegetables. Lessons can be offered in groups or one on one with a teacher and will include simple carving techniques including getting started and best fruits to use as a beginner, etc. Contact us for more info! You can contact us via the website contact form or finding our contact info in the footer of every page of this website.
