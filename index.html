Say it With Fruit... Expert Fruit Carving

This is our passion.

Say It With Fruit specializes in artistic fruit and vegetable carvings, platters, edible arrangements and fruit tables for any of your special, personal or corporate occasions. Our carvings are beautiful but also nutritious. We customize them to impress your guests and make your event unforgettable. It is not just food. We transform an ordinary fruit and veggies into pieces of art that are HEALTHY AND ALMOST TOO BEAUTIFUL TO EAT!!

COVID-19 Update: We are open to take orders for fruit and vegetables carvings and plates. Pick up only. Our carving lessons are organized one on one with a teacher in safe environment with all Covid-19 protocols in place.
