About Us

We specialize in spray foam and waterproofing installation for residential, commercial and industrial buildings. Years of experience results in us being able to flawlessly perform the services you need. Whether it's waterproofing, deep soil stabilization, soundproofing we hope to be your number one call.

We use only certified and the most advanced technologies to perform all of our services, ensuring that any Advanced Spray job will always have that quality touch to it.

Our Services

Spray Foam Insulation

Spray foam insulation works as a combination of air, moisture, pollutant and sound barrier that will keep your space comfortable.

Waterproofing

Keep your structures safe from water and moisture ingress using Advanced Spray's certified waterproofing methods and materials.

Air Barrier

Air barriers are an essential part to any residential, commercial or industrial building. Let us ensure that your buildings can control air leakage.

Blindside Waterproofing

Advanced waterproofing methods to maximize the size of foundations, but ensuring your structure stays completely waterproof.

Roofing

We use elastomeric coating for decks, roofs and metal surfaces. Our coating is weather resistant and waterproof to keep your structures intact.
