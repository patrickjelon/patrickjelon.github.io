Why get your car detailed?

Auto detailing is something every car owner should consider. Every single day the car’s paintwork picks up a huge number of contaminants. Whether it’s grime and tar, dead insects, brake dust, bird droppings or tree sap, any one of these can and will damage the paintwork on your precious asset. Removing these pollutants and contaminants will help preserve and maximize the resale value of your car. Who likes being surrounded by germs and bacteria? Studies show that cars, on average, have over 280 types of bacteria present in EVERY square inch of the vehicle, the nastiest being your steering wheel, shift knob, and trunk. Without question, the main reason why so much bacteria is collected in cars is because of how few people clean them well. Millions of germs, fungi, bacteria, and mold reside in a place where we spend so much time. How nasty!

Why Invest in a Quality Ceramic Coating?

A ceramic coating on your car is going to protect your vehicle against major contaminants and chemicals, scratches, harmful UV rays, and more. Many of us may not realize that the UV rays travel through the glass windows in our car and still damage our skin, potentially causing skin cancer. A ceramic coating is going to offer that UV protection, not only keeping you and your passengers safe, but also protecting your cars paint job from dullness and oxidization. The coating also intensifies the paint’s reflective properties causing your car to have an elevated glossy look and strong shine. Along with the great physical features, it is going to make your car hydrophobic. And when your car is able to repel water like that, it also makes it easier to keep your car clean, all the dirt and grime slides off easier. Other coatings and waxes only range a number of months, ceramic coating can last you up to 7 years. That is because the ceramic coat generates a bond with the paint of the car, therefore it doesn’t wash or break away like wax coating. It quite literally creates a new layer on your car, the paint job remains untouched, but it blends seamlessly into the car because of the bond. Investing in monthly waxes to make your car shine and get rid of scratches or stains, or whatever it may be, certainly adds up. Ceramic coating is more worth your money and time, but also protects your vehicle instantly and in the long run.
