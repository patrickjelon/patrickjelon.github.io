About the Book
The Truth Behind Your Failure

Skummy Life, a book about social economic systematic oppression. To put it simply, people are broke, and there’s a reason.

Skummy Life helps the individual see what’s truly happening, and how they’ve unknowingly been caught up in it. Skummy Life brings to light both the agenda and game plan that when worked in unison result in poverty. The book then helps the individual learn how to achieve the goals they have for themselves and their loved ones through intelligent hard work and by avoiding designed systematic pitfalls that they never knew were there to begin with.

Don't use this book as a tool to perpetuate a conspiratorial way of thinking, see it as a tool that will help validate the way you feel, open your eyes to systematic pitfalls, and understand the way society has been created to work. Treat this book as a tool that will help you gain the knowledge necessary to enable financial freedom and cure financial illness.
