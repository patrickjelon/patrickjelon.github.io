Instructions

  • Click "Add Quarterback" to add a quarterback.
  • Click "Add Receiver" to add receivers.
  • Click and drag players to move them around the field.
  • Click a player to draw a straight route, curve route, name them, or remove them.
  • Click ✓ to finish or × to cancel the route. U to undo last action.
  • To save your route as a PNG image, click the 'Save as PNG' button at the bottom of the screen.

Plans for the future: mobile responsiveness, route options (straight routes), double click to finish route, hot route / primary route label, defender markers, save routes to local storage
